Who We Are

Efoyita Refugee Support Initiative (ERSI) is a humanitarian organization dedicated to supporting refugees, youth, and women through psychosocial support, education, empowerment, and protection services.

Our Mission

To empower refugee youth and women by providing psychosocial support, creating safe spaces, and offering life skills trainings.

Our Vision

To see a safe and enabling environment where refugee youth and women are empowered, self-reliant, and living with dignity.

Our Core Values

Compassion • Accountability • Inclusiveness • Transparency • Empowerment

Our Programs

Psychosocial Support & Safe Spaces

We provide trauma-informed healing groups, counseling sessions, and safe communal environments for recovery and growth.

Education & Youth Empowerment

Ensuring youth have access to vital learning materials, educational mentorship programs, and core computer literacy skills.

Livelihood & Women's Protection

Empowering displaced women through tailoring, business development training, and financial independence circles.
